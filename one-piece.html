Story

Monkey D. Luffy ek young pirate hai jo Pirate King banne ka sapna dekhta hai. Usne Gum-Gum Devil Fruit khaya hai jiski wajah se uska body rubber ki tarah stretch ho sakta hai.

Luffy legendary treasure "One Piece" ko dhoondhne ke liye Grand Line mein safar shuru karta hai aur apni Straw Hat Pirates crew banata hai.

Safar ke dauran woh powerful pirates, marines aur dangerous enemies ka saamna karta hai.
