Story

Attack on Titan ek dark fantasy anime hai jahan insaniyat bahut bade aur khatarnak Titans ke darr mein jeeti hai. Log apni suraksha ke liye bahut badi deewaron ke andar rehte hain. Kai saalon tak shanti rehti hai, lekin ek din Colossal Titan deewar tod deta hai aur Titans shehar mein ghus aate hain.

Is attack mein Eren Yeager ki maa ki maut ho jati hai. Us din Eren kasam khata hai ki woh sabhi Titans ko khatam karega. Eren apne doston Mikasa Ackerman aur Armin Arlert ke saath Survey Corps join karta hai aur Titans ke khilaaf ladna shuru karta hai.

Jaise jaise kahani aage badhti hai, Titans ki asal sachchai, deewaron ke raaz aur duniya ke chhupe hue secrets saamne aate hain. Story action, mystery aur emotional moments se bhari hui hai.
