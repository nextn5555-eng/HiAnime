Story

Naruto Uzumaki Hidden Leaf Village ka ek young ninja hai. Uske andar powerful Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama) sealed hai. Is wajah se gaon ke log usse pasand nahi karte. Naruto sabko galat sabit karna chahta hai aur Hokage banne ka sapna dekhta hai.

Naruto mushkil missions, dangerous enemies aur powerful villains ka saamna karta hai. Apni mehnat aur doston ke saath woh dheere dheere duniya ke sabse powerful ninjas mein se ek ban jata hai.
