Story

Jujutsu Kaisen ki kahani Yuji Itadori ke around ghoomti hai, jo ek high school student hai aur bahut strong physical abilities rakhta hai. Ek din woh ek dangerous cursed object ke contact mein aata hai.

Apne doston ki jaan bachane ke liye Yuji Sukuna naam ke powerful Curse ki ungli nigal leta hai. Iske baad Sukuna uske andar aa jata hai aur Yuji ki zindagi hamesha ke liye badal jati hai.

Yuji ko Tokyo Jujutsu High bheja jata hai jahan woh Curses se ladna, Cursed Energy control karna aur duniya ko dangerous monsters se bachana seekhta hai.
